Who are we?

Bi-Be is a youth-focused organization dedicated to teaching students how to vibe code. We believe coding isn't just about syntax; it's about the feeling, the creativity, and the community.

Why Vibe Coding Matters

Vibe coding is all about removing the intimidation from programming. It's fast, it's expressive, and it allows you to bring your ideas to life with a flow that feels natural.
